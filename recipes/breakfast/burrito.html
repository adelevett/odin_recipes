Meet the Q-Rito!

Description

\

Start your morning off with a delicious and healthy treat.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 flour tortillas
  • Vegetables and spices of your chosing
  • Some oil

Steps

  1. Cook quinoa by heating it in water and simmering to evaporation after water reaches its boiling point
  2. Prepare vegetables spices in a sauce pan before adding the cooked quinoa along with some oil or vegan butter
  3. Warm tortillas in an oven to the edge of crispiness while still keeping them supple
  4. Fold quinoa and vegetable mixture into tortillas and fold them over to create a sealed pouch
